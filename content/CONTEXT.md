# Content Context

Scope: drafts and content assets in `content/`.

## Rules

- Treat these files as draft material, not live runtime truth.
- One article or draft per file.
- Keep slugs, filenames, and URLs ASCII-safe.
- Link to canonical live routes, not legacy slugs.
- Use the `pillar-cornerstone-writer` skill for repeatable long-form article packages.

## Boundary

Content drafts may inform repo work, but they do not replace editor-owned live content or system docs.
